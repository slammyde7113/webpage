About

I'm a passionate software developer who excels at solving tough problems. Programming has been a part of my life ever since I was kid, when I learned how to use cheat codes in the game "War Craft 3." I soon moved onward to building my own computers and then teaching myself how to code. Now I plan to make a career out of it!

In my down-time, my days are filled with binge watching tv shows, playing video games, and crafting campaigns in my weekly Dungeons and Dragons meet-up. I love reading books, especially those of the political or historical variety. I'm also no stranger to comic books. All in all, I'm a fun loving guy and I love trying new things, so if you have any recommendations for things to do in the Boston area, feel free to let me know!
